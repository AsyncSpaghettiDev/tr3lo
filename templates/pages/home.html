Tr3lo Manager

Welcome to Tr3lo Manager, a simple issue management tool.
We offer a solution to manage your issues and tasks in a simple way.
By using Tr3lo Manager you can create, edit, delete and assign issues to your team members.
We also offer a simple way to manage your projects and assign issues to them.
All you need to do is to register and start using our tool.
Is as simple as that!

How to create a task:

  • Click on the "Create Task" button
  • Fill in the form
  • Assing a status, by default is to do
  • Assign a priority (low, medium, high)
  • Click on the "Create" button
